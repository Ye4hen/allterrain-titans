BTR-90 “Rostok” (also designated as GAZ-5923) is a Russian armored personnel carrier. Combat wheeled floating armored vehicle, equipped with weapons, intended for use in motorized rifle units of the ground forces.<\/p>\n

The BTR-90 was presented to the public for the first time in 1994. The developer is OJSC Gorky Automobile Plant, the manufacturer is declared by OJSC Arzamas Machine-Building Plant, serial production was planned to begin in 2011.<\/p>\n

Tests of the armored personnel carrier were completed in 2004.<\/p>\n
